Replacing the heating and air in a larger Waxhaw home is a real investment, and the estate properties around Providence Downs and the older homes out past the horse farms often run more involved systems — multiple zones, variable-speed equipment, sometimes geothermal. That's the work we do best, and it's exactly where getting the design right pays off for years.

It's also where the markup games happen. Some companies see a big home off Providence Road West and quote the most expensive system on the lot, whether the house needs it or not. We start with a load calculation instead — square footage, ductwork, zoning, sun exposure — and recommend the equipment that actually fits. An oversized system blasts cold, shuts off fast, never pulls the humidity, and wears out early, no matter how premium the badge on it is.

## What's Included in Every Install

- A proper load calc and an honest equipment recommendation for your home and budget
- Full removal and haul-away of the old system
- New refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and thermostat or zoning controls
- System commissioning — we verify charge and airflow on every zone before we leave
- A walkthrough so you know how to run it
- A 2-year labor warranty, backed by the manufacturer's parts warranty, typically 10 years on major components when registered
