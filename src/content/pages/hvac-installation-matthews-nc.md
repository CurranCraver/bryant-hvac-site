In an older Matthews neighborhood, replacing the heating and air is less a question of if than when. Many of these homes are running systems installed 15, 20, even 25 years ago, and the R-22 refrigerant a lot of them use was phased out of production — so when an old system springs a leak, recharging it has gotten expensive enough that a new system often makes more sense. We help you weigh that honestly, not push you into it.

That honesty is the whole point, because this is where some companies get aggressive. They see an old system near the Matthews Sportsplex or out in Crestdale Crossing and quote the biggest, most expensive unit on the lot. We start with a load calculation instead — square footage, ductwork, orientation — and recommend the equipment that actually fits. An oversized system blasts cold, shuts off fast, never pulls the humidity, and wears out early.

## What's Included in Every Install

- A proper load calc and an honest equipment recommendation for your budget
- Full removal and haul-away of the old system, R-22 or otherwise
- New refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and thermostat setup
- System commissioning — we verify the charge and airflow before we leave
- A walkthrough so you know how to run it
- A 2-year labor warranty, backed by the manufacturer's parts warranty, typically 10 years on major components when registered
