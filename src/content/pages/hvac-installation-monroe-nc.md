Replacing your heating and air system is one of the bigger checks you'll write as a homeowner, and most people only do it once or twice in the time they own a house. So we treat it that way: we size it to your home, show you the options in plain numbers, and make sure you understand what you're buying before you commit to anything.

It starts with a load calculation, not a guess. A unit that's too big actually cools worse — it blasts cold, shuts off fast, and never runs long enough to pull the humidity out, so the house feels clammy and the equipment wears out early. The right size, matched to your square footage and ductwork, is what makes a system quiet, efficient, and long-lived.

## What's Included in Every Install

- A proper load calc and an honest equipment recommendation for your budget
- Full removal and haul-away of the old system
- New refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and thermostat setup
- System commissioning — we verify the charge and airflow before we leave
- A walkthrough so you know how to run it
- 2-year labor warranty, backed by the manufacturer's parts warranty (typically 10 years on major components when registered)
