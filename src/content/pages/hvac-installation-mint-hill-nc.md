Replacing your heating and air is one of the larger checks you'll write as a homeowner, and for a Mint Hill family it's also one you want to get right the first time — a system you can stop thinking about for the next fifteen years. So we treat it that way: we size it to your home, lay out the options in plain numbers, and make sure you understand what you're buying before you commit.

It starts with a load calculation, not a guess. The outfit that charges to show up and pushes the biggest, most expensive unit on the lot isn't doing you any favors; an oversized system blasts cold, shuts off fast, never pulls the humidity, and wears out early. The right size, matched to your square footage and ductwork, is what makes a system quiet, efficient, and long-lived.

## What's Included in Every Install

- A proper load calc and an honest equipment recommendation for your budget
- Full removal and haul-away of the old system
- New refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and thermostat setup
- System commissioning — we verify the charge and airflow before we leave
- A walkthrough so you know how to run it
- A 2-year labor warranty, backed by the manufacturer's parts warranty, typically 10 years on major components when registered
