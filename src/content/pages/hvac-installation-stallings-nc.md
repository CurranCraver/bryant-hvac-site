Replacing a heating and air system is one of the bigger checks you'll write, whether it's for a home off Old Monroe Road or a storefront along the US-74 corridor. Stallings is one of the few towns where we're handling both on the same street, so we treat each one the same way — size it to the building, lay out the options in plain numbers, and make sure you understand what you're buying before you commit.

That mix is also where some companies get loose with the quote. The outfit that charges to show up and pushes the biggest, most expensive unit on the lot isn't doing you any favors; an oversized system blasts cold, shuts off fast, never pulls the humidity, and wears out early. We start with a load calculation instead and recommend the equipment that actually fits.

## What's Included in Every Install

- A proper load calc and an honest equipment recommendation for your budget
- Full removal and haul-away of the old system
- New refrigerant lines, electrical connections, and thermostat setup
- System commissioning — we verify the charge and airflow before we leave
- A walkthrough so you know how to run it
- A 2-year labor warranty, backed by the manufacturer's parts warranty, typically 10 years on major components when registered
